Listening

Listening Section

The Listening section tests your ability to understand spoken English in academic and everyday settings. You will hear audio clips and answer 35 to 45 questions based on what you hear. There are three types of tasks in this section. Once you move forward, you will NOT be able to go back to earlier questions.

Type of Task Description
Listen and Choose a Response Pick the best answer to a short question or statement you hear.
Conversations Listen to a brief conversation and answer questions about it.
Announcements and Academic Talks Listen to announcements or lectures and respond to questions.
