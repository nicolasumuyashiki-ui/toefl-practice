Writing Section

The Writing section measures your ability to use written English in academic and everyday situations. You will complete three tasks. Once you finish a task, you will not be able to return to it.

Task TypeWhat You'll Do
Build a SentenceArrange words into grammatically correct responses (16 questions, 7 minutes).
Write an EmailRead a scenario and write an email response (1 task, 7 minutes).
Academic DiscussionRead a discussion thread and post your own response (1 task, 10 minutes).
⏱️ Each task is timed. The timer cannot be paused once started.
