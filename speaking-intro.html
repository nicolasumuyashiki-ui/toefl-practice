Speaking Section

The Speaking section measures your ability to speak English in academic and everyday situations. You will complete two tasks. Audio will play automatically and the response timer will start after each prompt.

Task TypeWhat You'll Do
Listen and RepeatListen to 7 sentences and repeat each one within 8–12 seconds.
Take an InterviewAnswer 4 interviewer questions, 45 seconds each.
🎤 Before you start, you will run a quick microphone check.
