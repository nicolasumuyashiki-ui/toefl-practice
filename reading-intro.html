Reading Section

In the Reading section, you will answer questions to demonstrate how well you understand academic and non-academic texts in English. You will read passages and respond to questions about them.

There are several types of tasks in this section:

Task Type What You'll Do
Complete the Words Type the missing letters to finish words in a short paragraph.
Read and Respond Read practical, everyday texts and respond to comprehension questions.
Academic Reading Read a longer academic text and answer questions about its content.

⚠️ You WILL NOT be able to return to previous questions.
